FAQ

Questions your customers will ask first.

A Point of Sale (POS) system is the software and hardware businesses use to process sales, accept payments, manage inventory, track customer purchases, and generate reports. Modern POS systems go beyond checkout by helping businesses manage daily operations from a single dashboard.

A modern POS system should include:

Fast checkout and payment processing
Inventory management
Sales reporting and analytics
Employee management
Customer profiles / loyalty programs
Receipt printing or digital receipts
Tax calculation
Multi-location support
Secure user permissions
Cloud backups and remote access

The best POS systems reduce manual work and improve business efficiency.

A secure POS should provide:

Encrypted payment processing
Password-protected user accounts
Role-based access control
Activity logs
Automatic backups
